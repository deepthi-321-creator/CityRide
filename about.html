About CityRide

CityRide is more than just a ride-booking platform — it's a local movement driven by trust, affordability, and ease. We’re here to make your daily commute smoother and more transparent with our community-first approach. Designed by drivers and loved by riders, CityRide connects you to reliable transport in seconds.

CityRide is a homegrown ride-hailing platform built with a mission to serve the people — by the people who know the roads best: the drivers themselves. Whether you're commuting to work, attending a family function, or catching a late-night train, CityRide ensures you reach safely, affordably, and on time.

We believe in fair pricing, verified drivers, and technology that empowers the community. Every ride you take supports hardworking local drivers and contributes to a more sustainable urban transport system.

Select vehicle, confirm fare & book — your ride is on the way!
